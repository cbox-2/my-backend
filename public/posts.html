تكامل المستخدم

إذا كان موقعك الإلكتروني يتضمن نظام تسجيل دخول خاص به، فيمكنك استخدام التكامل لتحقيق تسجيل دخول موحد. يستطيع المستخدمون تسجيل الدخول إلى موقعك، وسيتم تسجيل دخولهم تلقائيًا إلى حسابك على Cbox.

يمكن منح المستخدمين الذين تم التحقق من هويتهم عبر التكامل صلاحيات التعديل وغيرها من الصلاحيات في صفحة المستخدمين . فعّل التسجيل التلقائي لتجنب تسجيل أسماء المستخدمين يدويًا في صندوق التحكم الخاص بك لمنحهم الصلاحيات.

إذا كان موقعك يعتمد على لغة PHP، فقم بتعديل كود تضمين Cbox بالطريقة التالية: 

...&sec=form&nme=<?php echo urlencode($name)?>&nmekey=<?php echo md5('8i9if550i3k4a2ck'.$name)?>

عند ظهور المتغير $name، أدخل المتغير الذي يمثل اسم المستخدم كما تم استرجاعه من قاعدة بيانات المستخدمين لديك. سيختلف هذا باختلاف نظام إدارة المحتوى أو نظام المنتدى الذي تستخدمه.

You'll need to have an ExpressionEngine-based website up and running before proceeding with these steps. Once you have ExpressionEngine running it's very easy to get Cbox integrated with your userbase. If you have already installed your Cbox in your site template, you can skip the following steps and go straight to the Integration guide.

Standard installation guide:

  1. Log in to your ExpressionEngine Control Panel.
  2. Go to the Templates section.
  3. Click your index template to edit it.
  4. Choose the location for your Cbox. You may want to place it somewhere after the tag 
    <div id="sidebar">
  5. Paste your Cbox HTML code and click Update.

Confirm your Cbox appears on your site before proceeding with integration:

Integration guide:

  1. Download our Cbox on ExpressionEngine module to your computer.
  2. Extract the module files to the ./system directory in your ExpressionEngine installation (this may have been renamed). Note that the mcp.cbox.php and mod.cbox.php files go in the ./system/modules directory, and lang.cbox.php goes in the ./system/language directory.
  3. Log in to your ExpressionEngine Control Panel.
  4. Choose the Modules section. Cbox should be in the list of modules.
  5. Click Install for the Cbox module.
  6. Now go to the Templates section.
  7. Click to edit the template in which your Cbox is installed (by default, index).
  8. Locate your Cbox HTML code in your template.
  9. Find (with Ctrl+F): 
    <!-- BEGIN CBOX ... -->
    and paste before it: 
    {exp:cbox privkey="8i9if550i3k4a2ck"}
  10. Find: 
    <!-- END CBOX -->
    and paste after it:
    {/exp:cbox}
  11. Find: 
    sec=form
    and immediately after it, paste: 
    &nme={name}&nmekey={key}
  12. Click Update to save your Template.
  13. And finally, click Enable lower down on this page to turn user integration on.

You'll need to have a Joomla-based website up and running before proceeding with these steps. Once you have Joomla running it's very easy to get Cbox integrated with your Joomla userbase. Here are the steps:

  1. First download our Cbox on Joomla module to your computer.
  2. In your Joomla administrator menu, go to Extensions -> Install/Uninstall
  3. Use the Upload Package File tool to upload the Cbox on Joomla module you downloaded from us.
  4. Then go to Extensions -> Module Manager and click on Cbox on the list that appears.
  5. Now set Enabled to Yes, and paste your snippet (from here) and private key (see below, required for integration) into the parameter fields on the right
  6. Click Save
  7. And finally, click Enable lower down on this page to turn user integration on.

These steps will integrate your Cbox with your phpBB3 userbase.

  1. Navigate to a copy of your phpbb installation directory.
  2. Open the file /includes/functions.php in a plain-text editor such as Notepad.
  3. Find (using Ctrl+F): 
    		$l_login_logout = sprintf($user->lang['LOGOUT_USER'], $user->data['username']);
  4. On the next line, add: 
    		$cbox_username = urlencode($user->data['username']);
		$cbox_userkey = md5('8i9if550i3k4a2ck'.$user->data['username']);
  5. Then find: 
    		'BOARD_URL'			=> $board_url,
  6. On the next line, add: 
    		'CBOX_USERNAME'		=> $cbox_username,
		'CBOX_USERKEY'		=> $cbox_userkey,
  7. Save and upload /includes/functions.php to your phpbb installation directory on your server.
  8. Go into your phpBB admin control panel.
  9. Click the Styles tab, choose the Templates side bar item and for the current template, click Edit.
  10. If your Cbox is installed already, choose the template file in which you placed it. If Cbox is not installed, choose a location. Recommended files are overall_footer.html or overall_header.html.
  11. Paste your Cbox HTML code into the template file. (Your Cbox HTML code is at this page.)
  12. In your Cbox HTML code, find: sec=form and immediately after, paste: &nme={CBOX_USERNAME}&nmekey={CBOX_USERKEY}
  13. Important: remove the <!-- BEGIN CBOX --> and <!-- END CBOX --> tags from your code. These will cause an error on your template.
  14. Save.
  15. Click Enable lower down on this page to turn user integration on.
  16. Refresh your forum to confirm you are logged in to your Cbox.

You'll need to have a Simple Machines Forum-based website up and running before proceeding with these steps. The Cbox SMF module will get Cbox on your SMF template easily, and it'll also integrate your SMF users with Cbox.

  1. First download our Cbox on SMF module to your computer.
  2. Go to the SMF Administration, and click on Packages.
  3. Click on the Download Packages tab.
  4. At Upload a Package at the bottom of the page, choose the mod_cbox_smf11.tar.gz file you just downloaded, and click Upload.
  5. The message "Package has been downloaded successfully" is displayed. Click Apply Mod beneath it.
  6. Click Install Now. You should then see "The package was installed successfully".
  7. Go to Features and Options under the Configuration heading on the menu to your left (or click on Features and Options on the Admin main page).
  8. Scroll down to locate the Cbox options. Paste your code snippet (from here), private key (see below, required for integration) and choose where to place the box (header or footer).
  9. Save changes.
  10. And finally, click Enable lower down on this page to turn user integration on.

You'll need to have a vBulletin-based website up and running with your Cbox already installed in your template, before proceeding with these steps.

  1. Log in to your vBulletin Admin Control Panel.
  2. Choose Plugins & Products -> Add New Plugin from the menu.
  3. Create a new plugin at 'forumhome_complete' and insert this code: 
    $cbox_nme = urlencode($vbulletin->userinfo['username']);
$cbox_key = md5('8i9if550i3k4a2ck' . $vbulletin->userinfo['username']);
  4. Save.
  5. Locate your Cbox HTML code in your forum template.
  6. In your Cbox HTML code, find: sec=form and immediately after, paste: 
    &nme=$cbox_nme&nmekey=$cbox_key
  7. Save.
  8. And finally, click Enable lower down on this page to turn user integration on.

You'll need to have a Wordpress-based website up and running before proceeding with these steps.

  1. Open your Wordpress Theme Editor (Administration > Appearance > Editor).
  2. Paste the following into the template your want your Cbox to appear in: 
    <?php global $user_ID, $user_identity ?>

<?php if ( $user_ID ) : ?>
[your Cbox HTML code]
<?php endif;  ?>
  3. Where it says [your Cbox HTML code] above, replace with your Cbox HTML code.
  4. In your Cbox HTML code, find: sec=form and immediately after, paste: 
    &nme=<?php echo urlencode($user_identity)?>&nmekey=<?php echo md5('8i9if550i3k4a2ck'.$user_identity)?>
  5. Save.
  6. And finally, click Enable lower down on this page to turn user integration on.
  7. If you want unregistered (guest) visitors to be able to see your Cbox as well, delete the lines: 
    <?php if ( $user_ID ) : ?>
    and 
    <?php endif;  ?>

بعد تفعيل التكامل، اختبر عملية تسجيل الدخول على موقعك الإلكتروني وتأكد من إمكانية النشر على صندوق بريدك باستخدام الاسم المسجل به في موقعك. إذا ظهرت لك رسالة تفيد باحتمالية وجود خلل في إعدادات صندوق بريدك، فانقر على "تعطيل" أعلاه وأعد التحقق من التثبيت. إذا استمرت المشكلة، تواصل معنا .

تكامل موسع

يمكنك توسيع نطاق التكامل بإضافة صور المستخدمين الشخصية وروابط ملفاتهم الشخصية بالإضافة إلى أسمائهم. الصق الكود التالي بعد تطبيق كود التكامل القياسي: 

&pic=<?php echo urlencode($avatar_url)?>&lnk=<?php echo urlencode($profile_url)?>&ekey=<?php echo md5("8i9if550i3k4a2ck".chr(9).$avatar_url.chr(9).$profile_url)?>

غيّر قيمتي $avatar_url و$profile_url إلى القيم الصحيحة للسياق. لاحظ أن كلتيهما تظهران مرتين. ستحتاج إلى تفعيل حقل البريد الإلكتروني/عنوان URL والصور الرمزية في خيارات النشر إذا لم تكن قد فعلت ذلك بالفعل.
